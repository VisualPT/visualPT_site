Our Values

  • Data-driven, analytical - Visual data is rich and untapped. Better tools are needed. The PT profession currently uses intuitive, manual methods to capture and record this data that are slow and difficult to retrieve. We want to use new, data discovery methods to improve physical therapist decision making.
  • Empathic, not sympathetic - “Healthcare is broken” or “US healthcare is too expensive” - it would be easy to fall into existing tropes that judge and criticize (and frequently, politicize) healthcare spending in the United States. Our team understands healthcare professionals face difficult decisions every day. We want to use data to help them make better decisions.
  • Hard on the process; easy on the person - We're a small team, figuring stuff out in a pretty significant whitespace. We have fun and we enjoy the process.
